# Learning Record 0006: OWASP RRM Foundations

## Date
2026-06-14

## Context
A dedicated introductory lesson on OWASP Risk Rating Methodology (RRM) was requested because the learner is not yet familiar with it.

## Key Insights
- OWASP RRM provides a structured likelihood x impact approach with factor-level reasoning.
- RRM is a base framework and should be customized for organizational context.
- RRM can enrich deterministic local scoring by improving the quality of likelihood and impact estimation.
- Confidence and uncertainty handling still need to be modeled explicitly in design-phase workflows.

## Working Rules
- Use RRM factors to derive better inputs, not to replace local governance thresholds.
- Prioritize business impact over technical impact when business data is available.
- Preserve deterministic scoring bands and escalation rules for consistency.

## Implications For Future Lessons
- Next practical sessions should compare base scoring versus RRM-enriched scoring on the same scenarios.
- Calibration guidance should be revisited regularly to reduce reviewer drift.
