# Mission: AI-Assisted Threat Modelling for Security Reviews

## Why You're Learning This

You lead security reviews and want to harness AI as a threat modelling partner while developing a sharp threat identification mindset. Rather than replacing human judgment, you're aiming to use AI to amplify your ability to identify and reason about security risks.

## Context
- **Professional Goal**: Leading security reviews with structured, repeatable threat identification
- **Approach**: Learning both traditional human-driven threat modelling AND how to effectively use AI to help surface threats
- **Focus**: Building the mindset for threat identification—the ability to think like an attacker and spot weaknesses systematically
- **Current Level**: Intermediate—you understand the basics of threat modelling, now deepening

## What Success Looks Like
- You can conduct a threat model manually, reasoning through attack vectors systematically
- You know how to brief an AI effectively to help identify threats it might miss on its own
- You've internalized the threat identification mindset so you can catch what AI misses
- You can lead security reviews with confidence, using AI as a tool rather than a crutch
