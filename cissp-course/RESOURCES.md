# CISSP Resources

## Knowledge

- **Book: _ISC2 CISSP Official Study Guide, 10th Edition_ — Chapple, Stewart, Gibson (Wiley/Sybex, 2024)**
  The primary study text for this workspace. 21 chapters across all 8 domains. Use for: authoritative topic coverage, chapter review questions, Study Essentials summaries.
  _Local copy: `D:\Playground\Security\Book\CISSP_OG_10th_Edition.md`_

- **Book: _ISC2 CISSP Official Practice Tests, 4th Edition_ — Chapple & Seidl (Wiley/Sybex, 2024)**
  Companion practice test book. Contains 8 domain-specific question sets (one per domain) + 4 full 125-question practice exams + full answer explanations. Use for: end-of-phase retrieval practice, domain self-assessment (Lesson 0001 diagnostic), and mock exams in Phase 5 (Weeks 23–26).
  _Local copy: `D:\Playground\Security\Book\CISSP_OG_10th_Edition_Test.md`_
  _Structure: Ch 1–8 = domain tests · Ch 9–12 = Practice Tests 1–4 · Appendix = all answers_

- **[ISC2 Exam Guidance for Artificial Intelligence (April 2, 2026)](https://edge.sitecorecloud.io/internationf173-xmc4e73-prodbc0f-9660/media/Project/ISC2/Main/Media/exam-guidance/ISC2-Exam-Guidance.pdf)**
  ⚠️ **Required supplement** — AI concepts are now live in the CISSP exam. This free public PDF maps exactly where AI security topics appear across all 8 CISSP domains. Your 10th Edition study guide (2024) predates this guidance. Read this before Phase 1 begins. Use for: identifying AI-specific exam topics per domain, calibrating lesson AI callouts.

- [ISC2 CISSP Exam Outline (official CBK)](https://www.isc2.org/certifications/cissp/cissp-cbk)
  The authoritative source of what is and isn't on the exam. Use for: confirming which sub-topics are in scope, checking domain weightings.

- [ISC2 CISSP CAT Exam FAQ](https://www.isc2.org/certifications/cissp/cissp-cat)
  Official explanation of the Computerized Adaptive Testing format. Use for: exam strategy, understanding how scoring works.

- [Mike Chapple's CertMike Study Groups](https://www.certmike.com)
  Free study group content from the primary author of the Official Study Guide. Use for: supplemental video walkthroughs, exam tips.

- [NIST SP 800 series (nist.gov)](https://csrc.nist.gov/publications/sp800)
  Primary source for risk management frameworks (NIST RMF, SP 800-37, SP 800-53). Use for: Domain 1 depth — risk frameworks, control families.

- **[NIST AI Risk Management Framework (AI RMF 1.0)](https://airc.nist.gov/RMF)**
  ⚠️ **Required for D1 and D3** — The NIST AI RMF defines four core functions (Govern, Map, Measure, Manage) for managing AI risk. Now in scope for CISSP per the April 2026 ISC2 guidance. Use for: Lesson 0002 (AI primer), D1 risk management lessons, D3 architecture lessons.

- **[EU AI Act (official text summary)](https://digital-strategy.ec.europa.eu/en/policies/regulatory-framework-ai)**
  ⚠️ **Required for D1** — EU regulation classifying AI systems by risk tier (Unacceptable → High → Limited → Minimal). Analogous to GDPR for AI governance. Now in scope for CISSP per ISC2 April 2026 guidance. Use for: governance and compliance questions in Domain 1 lessons.

- [NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF 2.0)](https://www.nist.gov/cyberframework)
  Use for: Domain 1 governance context; how the CSF maps to CISSP risk concepts.

- [RFC index (IETF)](https://www.ietf.org/rfc/)
  Primary source for protocol specifications (TLS, IPsec, DNS). Use for: Domain 4 (Network Security) technical depth.

## Wisdom (Communities)

- [r/cissp (Reddit)](https://www.reddit.com/r/cissp/)
  Active community of candidates and CISSPs. High signal-to-noise for exam prep strategy, mindset questions ("think like a manager"), and domain-specific confusion. Use for: peer perspective, reality-checking exam approach.

- [ISC2 Community Forums](https://community.isc2.org/)
  Official forum. Use for: clarifying exam policies, CPE requirements, endorsement process questions.

- [LinkedIn CISSP Study Group](https://www.linkedin.com/groups/73701/)
  Professional network of CISSP holders. Use for: career questions, "how is this used in practice?" wisdom.

## Gaps

- No dedicated resource yet for Domain 8 (Software Development Security) depth beyond the study guide — explore OWASP and CSSLP materials.
- Physical security (Domain 3, Ch 10) has limited free primary sources — the study guide is likely sufficient.
- Practice Tests (4th Ed, 2024) predate the April 2026 AI guidance — mock exam AI questions may be underrepresented; supplement with ISC2 community forums and any new official sample questions.
